"The righteous cry out, and the LORD hears them; he delivers them from all their troubles. The LORD is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit."

Deliverance Church - Mateka

Founded on the Word of God

We are rooted in the teachings of the Bible, seeking guidance and wisdom from Scripture.

Apostolic in Governance

Our church is led by apostolic principles, emphasizing strong leadership and spiritual authority.

Pentecostal in Practice

We embrace the Pentecostal tradition, experiencing the power and gifts of the Holy Spirit.

Indigenous in Foundation

Our church has deep roots in the local community, valuing cultural diversity and heritage.

Evangelistic in Approach

We are committed to spreading the Gospel and sharing the love of Christ with the world.

Autonomous in Operation

Each congregation has the freedom to govern itself while remaining united in faith.

Transformative in Community

We strive to make a positive impact on our community, serving and supporting those in need.
