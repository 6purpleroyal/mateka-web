About Us

VISION

  • The Church of Choice. Transforming Nations for Jesus Christ

MISSION

  • To Reach out, Equip, and Affect generations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

CORE VALUES

  • Hospitality
  • Excellence
  • Teamwork
  • Transparency
  • Accountability

OBJECTIVES

  1. To preach and promote the gospel of Jesus Christ through evangelism and discipleship
  2. To raise funds locally and internationally to achieve the church objectives
  3. To promote social welfare and concern within the community through households care support
  4. To establish and manage academic institutions
  5. To establish and manage health facilities

Responsibilities of the Local Church Council

  1. Opening, running, and closing of the church bank accounts.
  2. Ensure updated audited books of accounts are maintained.
  3. Implement the vision, mission, and policy guidelines of the church.
  4. Formulating guidelines on the daily running of the church.
  5. Advise on the recruitment, employment, remuneration, and discharge of the church staff.

Church Projects

  • Purchase of Land
  • Support of Vulnerable Children
